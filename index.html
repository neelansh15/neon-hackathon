About

KJSCE Hack brings together a group of inspired and creative coders from all across India. We present to them a platform where they can showcase their talent in the spirit of progress and technology. Most engineers lack hands-on experience on industrial problem solving. Traditionally conducted as an in-person hackathon, KJSCE Hack is the rostrum for coders and engineers to exercise their skills, work towards solving real industry level problems and win prizes - all over the course of 24 hours.

This hackathon is your chance at creating something impactful, something real! Owing to the pandemic, KJSCE Hack will be conducted this year as an Online Hackathon.
